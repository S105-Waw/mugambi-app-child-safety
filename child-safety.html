Child Safety & Protection Standards

The Mugambi App is committed to protecting children and preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

We maintain a zero-tolerance policy toward any content or behavior that exploits, abuses, or endangers children.

Prohibited Content and Behavior

  • Sexual content involving minors
  • Grooming, solicitation, or exploitation of children
  • Sharing, requesting, or distributing child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Any activity intended to harm, manipulate, or exploit minors

Safety Measures

  • User-generated content may be reviewed and moderated
  • Users can report inappropriate content or behavior
  • Accounts involved in CSAE-related activities are permanently banned
  • We cooperate with law enforcement and relevant authorities when required

Age Restriction

The Mugambi App is intended for users aged 18 years and above.
